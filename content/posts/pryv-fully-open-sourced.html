March 2025

Pryv is a Swiss-based non-for-profit associationas per Article 60 of the "Code civil suisse" with the following purpose

  • Maintain and develop Pryv.io as an open-source software.
  • Publicize and promote the Pryv.io software free of charge.
  • Organize and lead a community of interest around the Pryv.io software
Pryv association status are available online -here- (in French).
