Pryv.io software is now fully open-sourced and recognized as a Digital Public Good by the Digital Public Goods Alliance, a UN-endorsed initiative promoting open-source technologies that advance the Sustainable Development Goals.

Over the past decade, Pryv.io has evolved through collaborations with hospitals, pharmaceutical companies, medtech, biotech, and insurtech sectors, serving as a catalyst for patient-centric, data-driven healthcare solutions.

With Pryv.io now fully open-source, we're not just sharing technology — we're sharing the values of privacy, trust, and empowerment. This journey is about more than healthcare; it's about building tools that respect individuals in every aspect of life, across industries and borders.

Led by the Pryv Association, we are profoundly grateful to everyone who has been a part of this journey. Together, we're creating something much bigger — a future where personal data is managed ethically, collaboratively, and transparently.

We would like to thank Pryv's investors, customers and the continuous support of the Canton de Vaud.
