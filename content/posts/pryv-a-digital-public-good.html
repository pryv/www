April 2024

The DPGAlliance granted the status of Digital public good to Open-Pryv.io
With the description:
Personal Data & Privacy Management Software A ready-to-use solution for personal data and consent management. Pryv.io is a solid foundation on which you build your own digital health solution, so you can collect, store, share and rightfully use personal data.

Open-Pryv.io on DPGAlliance website.
