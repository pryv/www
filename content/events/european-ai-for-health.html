Pryv participates to the AI for Health is the European leading AI for Healthcare initiative which links expertise to design our vision of tomorrow’s European healthcare.

The European AI for Health aims to address the issue by gathering all the actors from the worlds of science, technology, laboratories, major groups and politicians on a European scale.

This conference is part of the AI for Good initiative, which objective is to respond, through Artificial Intelligence, to the 17 Sustainable Development Goals of the United Nations.
