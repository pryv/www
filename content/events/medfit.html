Held on the 25th and 26th of June 2019 in Lille – France, and gathering 700 international participants from over 25 countries, MedFIT is the leading European business convention dedicated to innovation partnerships in the fields of medical technology, diagnostics and digital health sector.

MedFIT is the place-to-be to identify innovative products and technologies, to build R&amp;D, find
licensing opportunities and financial partnerships.

MedFIT brings together industry leaders, innovative MedTechs, diagnostics start-ups, TTOs, academic
institutions, R&amp;D services as well as companies and investors to help facilitate market access for
innovations.

MedFIT features:
Business convention : schedule one-to-one pre-qualified meetings
Conference program : discuss collaboration, financing, market access and digitalization
Pitch sessions : sessions promoting innovative start-ups, licensing opportunity presentations
as well as service

Meet the experts sessions : address all your strategic needs to specific experts
Exhibition : showcase companies
Networking evening
