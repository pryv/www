Hacking Health – 3ème marathon de l’innovation ouverte en santé

Do not miss the opportunity to meet Francois Recorbet and attend his keynote speech at the HackingHealth event in Besançon.

He will share with you the inspiring story of how Pryv was born and what it takes to be positioned today as a European leading provider of privacy and personal data management software.
Furthermore, you will learn about must-apply rules in managing personal data in your Medtech and digital health projects.

The confidentiality of personal data is an enabler to differentiate your products and services, foster your innovations and build trust with your users. Make sure that you go one step further and benefit from turning data privacy compliance into a business competitive advantage.
